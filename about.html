Our Story

Fragranze.lk was founded in 2020 with a singular vision: to bring the world's most exquisite fragrances to the discerning noses of Sri Lanka. What began as a small passion project has blossomed into the island's premier destination for luxury perfumes.

We believe that fragrance is more than just a scent—it's an expression of personality, a memory captured in a bottle, and an invisible accessory that completes your presence. Our curated collection spans from timeless classics to modern masterpieces, ensuring every customer finds their perfect signature scent.

Every bottle in our collection is 100% authentic, sourced directly from authorized distributors and brand houses. We take pride in our rigorous quality standards and our commitment to delivering only genuine luxury fragrances to our customers.
