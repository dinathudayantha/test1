Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Are your perfumes 100% authentic?

Absolutely! All our perfumes are sourced directly from authorized distributors and brand houses. We guarantee 100% authenticity on every product we sell. Each item comes with original packaging and batch codes for verification.

How long does delivery take?

We offer same-day delivery within Colombo city limits. For other districts, delivery typically takes 2-3 business days. Free delivery is available on all orders over LKR 5,000.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return unopened and unused products in their original packaging within 7 days of delivery for a full refund.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery?

Yes! We offer Cash on Delivery (COD) across Sri Lanka. You can also pay via Visa, MasterCard, or Bank Transfer for online orders.
